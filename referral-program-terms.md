5. Incentives
5.1 New Customer Discount. A Referred Customer who applies a valid Referral Code prior to completing their first qualifying purchase will receive a discount of twenty percent (20%) off the total value of Base Credits in that transaction ("New Customer Discount"), subject to the $100.00 USD minimum purchase requirement in Section 3.3(c) and up to a maximum discount of two thousand U.S. dollars ($2,000.00 USD) (the "New Customer Discount Cap"). For the avoidance of doubt, where twenty percent (20%) of the total value of Base Credits in a qualifying transaction would exceed the New Customer Discount Cap, the discount shall be fixed at $2,000.00 USD and the Referred Customer shall be responsible for the remaining balance at the standard purchase price. The New Customer Discount:
(a) Applies to the Referred Customer's first qualifying purchase only, even if the Referred Customer receives separate Referral Codes from separate Referrers, and may not be applied retroactively;
(b) Applies to total value of Base Credits only; it does not independently affect the calculation or eligibility of Volume Bonus Credits, which are awarded separately pursuant to Company’s standard volume bonus schedule based on the pre-discount transaction value;
(c) Is non-transferable, has no cash value, and may not be combined with other promotional offers or discounts unless expressly stated by Company; 
(d) Is subject to a minimum transaction value of $100.00 USD in Base Credits prior to application of the discount; and
(e) Is subject to the New Customer Discount Cap of $2,000.00 USD per qualifying transaction. The New Customer Discount Cap implies that the discount applies in full only on transactions up to $10,000.00 USD in total value of Base Credits; transactions exceeding $10,000.00 USD in Base Credit value will receive a fixed discount of $2,000.00 USD rather than a full 20% reduction;
5.2 Volume Bonus Credits for Referred Customers. Referred Customers remain eligible to receive Volume Bonus Credits on their first qualifying purchase in accordance with Company's standard volume-based credit bonus schedule, applied in the usual manner. The New Customer Discount does not disqualify a Referred Customer from receiving Volume Bonus Credits.
5.3 Referrer Bonus.
(a) Calculation. Upon a Referred Customer completing a qualifying first purchase and Company receiving Payment in full for that transaction, the Referrer will receive a credit bonus equal to twenty percent (20%) of the Base Credits purchased by the Referred Customer in that transaction (“Referral Bonus”), up to a maximum of 200,000 credits per qualifying referral transaction ("Referral Bonus Cap"). Where twenty percent (20%) of the Base Credits purchased would otherwise exceed the Referral Bonus Cap, the Referral Bonus shall be fixed at 200,000 Credits regardless of the transaction value. For the avoidance of doubt, the Referral Bonus is calculated on Base Credits only and does not include any Volume Bonus Credits awarded to the Referred Customer in connection with that transaction.
Example: A Referred Customer purchases 50,000 in Base Credits. The Referrer's Referral Bonus is 20% × 50,000 = 10,000 in Credits. In another case, a Referred Customer purchases 5,000,000 in Base Credits. Notwithstanding that 20% × 5,000,000 = 1,000,000 in Credits, the Referrer's Referral Bonus is fixed at 200,000 Credits pursuant to the Referral Bonus Cap.
(b) Timing — General. Referral Bonuses will be processed and credited to the Referrer's account within five (5) business days following Company's receipt of Payment from the Referred Customer's qualifying purchase. Referral Bonuses will not be issued until Payment has been confirmed to be received by Company. For credit card transactions, this typically occurs within 5 business days of the transaction date. For purchase order transactions, this occurs upon receipt of cleared payment following applicable vendor registration and payment terms, which may extend thirty (30) days or longer from the invoice date. These are only estimates, may vary, and are subject to change. Company assumes no obligation to issue a Referral Bonus until receipt of Payment is confirmed, regardless of purchase order issuance date.
(c) Minimum Purchase Threshold. No Referral Bonus will be issued in connection with a Referred Customer's first purchase unless the total transaction value equals or exceeds $100.00 USD in Base Credits (prior to application of the New Customer Discount).
(d) Account in Good Standing. The Referral Bonus will only be credited if the Referrer's account remains active and in good standing at the time of crediting.
(e) Clawback. If a Referred Customer's qualifying purchase is refunded, reversed, charged back, or otherwise voided after a Referral Bonus has been credited or if a Referral Bonus is found to have been awarded erroneously or due to fraud, prohibited conduct, misuse, or other behavior in violation of these Referral Terms, Company reserves the right to reverse, deduct, or offset the corresponding Referral Bonus from the Referrer's account. If the Referral Bonus has been partially or fully utilized, we may deduct the equivalent value from the Referrer's existing Credit balance or apply an offset against future Credits.
(f) Credit Nature. Referral Bonuses are issued as Platform Credits. They are non-transferable, have no cash redemption value unless otherwise required by applicable law, and are subject to the Credit terms set forth in the General Agreements, including any applicable expiration terms.
