5. Incentives

  - 5.1 New Customer Discount. A Referred Customer who applies a valid Referral Code prior to completing their first qualifying purchase will receive a discount of twenty percent (20%) off the total value of Base Credits in that transaction ("New Customer Discount"), subject to the $100 minimum purchase requirement in Section 3.3(c). The New Customer Discount:
(a) Applies to the Referred Customer's first qualifying purchase only and may not be applied retroactively;
(b) Applies to Base Credits only; it does not independently affect the calculation or eligibility of Volume Bonus Credits, which are awarded separately pursuant to Company’s standard volume bonus schedule based on the pre-discount transaction value;
(c) Is non-transferable, has no cash value, and may not be combined with other promotional offers or discounts unless expressly stated by Company; and
(d) Is subject to a minimum transaction value of USD $100.00 in Base Credits prior to application of the discount.
  - 5.2 Volume Bonus Credits for Referred Customers. Referred Customers remain eligible to receive Volume Bonus Credits on their first qualifying purchase in accordance with Company's standard volume-based credit bonus schedule, applied in the usual manner. The New Customer Discount does not disqualify a Referred Customer from receiving Volume Bonus Credits.
  - 5.3 Referrer Bonus.
(a) Calculation. Upon a Referred Customer completing a qualifying first purchase and Company receiving Payment in full for that transaction, the Referrer will receive a credit bonus equal to twenty percent (20%) of the Base Credits purchased by the Referred Customer in that transaction ("Referral Bonus"). For the avoidance of doubt, the Referral Bonus is calculated on Base Credits only and does not include any Volume Bonus Credits awarded to the Referred Customer in connection with that transaction.
Example: A Referred Customer purchases 100,000 in Base Credits and qualifies for 5,000 Volume Bonus Credits under the standard schedule. The Referrer's Referral Bonus is 20% × 100,000 = 20,000 in Credits. The Referrer does not receive any portion of the 5,000 in Volume Bonus Credits.
(b) Timing — General. Referral Bonuses will be processed and credited to the Referrer's account within five (5) business days following Company's receipt of Payment from the Referred Customer's qualifying purchase. Referral Bonuses will not be issued until Payment Received has been confirmed.
(c) Timing — Purchase Orders. Where a Referred Customer's qualifying purchase is fulfilled via purchase order, processing times may be extended beyond five (5) business days due to vendor registration requirements and applicable payment terms. Referral Bonuses associated with purchase order transactions will be credited within five (5) business days of confirmed Payment Received, which may occur thirty (30) or more days following the transaction date. Company assumes no obligation to issue a Referral Bonus until Payment Received is confirmed, regardless of purchase order issuance date.
(d) Minimum Purchase Threshold. No Referral Bonus will be issued in connection with a Referred Customer's first purchase unless the total transaction value equals or exceeds USD $100.00 in Base Credits (prior to application of the New Customer Discount).
(e) Annual Cap. The total value of Referral Bonuses credited to any single account shall not exceed USD $600.00 per calendar year ("Annual Cap"), regardless of the number of members associated with that account or the number of successful referrals completed. Once an account reaches the Annual Cap in any calendar year, no further Referral Bonuses will be credited to that account for the remainder of that calendar year. The Annual Cap resets on January 1 of each subsequent calendar year.
(f) Multi-Member Accounts. Where an account has multiple members, the Annual Cap applies at the account level, not the individual member level. Referral Bonuses generated by any member of an account aggregate toward the single account-level Annual Cap.
(g) Account in Good Standing. The Referral Bonus will only be credited if the Referrer's account remains active and in good standing at the time of crediting.
(h) Clawback. If a Referred Customer's qualifying purchase is refunded, reversed, charged back, or otherwise voided after a Referral Bonus has been credited, Company reserves the right to reverse, deduct, or offset the corresponding Referral Bonus from the Referrer's account. If the Referral Bonus has been partially or fully utilized, we may deduct the equivalent value from the Referrer's existing Credit balance or apply an offset against future Credits.
(i) Credit Nature. Referral Bonuses are issued as Platform Credits. They are non-transferable, have no cash redemption value unless otherwise required by applicable law, and are subject to the Credit terms set forth in the General Terms, including any applicable expiration terms.
